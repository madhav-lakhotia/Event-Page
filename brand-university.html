Brand Your University 2K25

About the Event

Showcase Poornima University's branding by creating an engaging **2-3 minute video** that highlights the university's infrastructure, student life, and tech fest activities.

Team & Video Specifications

  • ⭐ **Each team must have 3 members.**
  • ⭐ **Video Duration:** 2-3 minutes.
  • ⭐ **Focus:** University branding, tech fest highlights, and student experiences.
  • ⭐ **Tone:** Creative, positive, and respectful.

Event Guidelines

  • 📌 Videos must cover various **tech fest activities, exhibitions, and student interactions.**
  • 📌 **Deadline:** Submit the final video via Google Form by **02:00 PM**.
  • 📌 **Winning Criteria:** Creativity, uniqueness, and quality judged by a panel.
  • 📌 **Top videos** may be showcased on the university’s official social media pages.
  • 📌 **Respect for all participants is mandatory.** Unsportsmanlike conduct will lead to disqualification.
