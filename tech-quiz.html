Tech Quiz Challenge

About the Quiz

The Tech Quiz Challenge is a thrilling competition designed to test participants' knowledge of technology, software, hardware, and current trends in the tech industry.

General Rules

  • ★ Teams must consist of 2 participants each.
  • ★ Participants cannot discuss answers with other teams.
  • ★ In case of a dispute, the coordinator's decision is final.
  • ★ In case of a tie, a bonus tiebreaker challenge will be conducted.
  • ★ The quiz consists of 3 rounds.

📍 Round 1: Technical Questions

  • ★ Each correct answer: +3 points.
  • ★ Incorrect answer: -2 points.
  • ★ If all teams fail, 4 options will be given. Correct answer: +2 points.
  • ★ If a team raises their hand but fails to answer in 10 seconds: -1 point.
  • ★ 10 teams will be eliminated in this round.

📍 Round 2: Who Am I?

  • ★ Participants identify a tech personality using clues.
  • ★ Answering after 1st clue: +5 points.
  • ★ Points decrease after each additional clue.
  • ★ Last clue answer: +1 point.
  • ★ Incorrect answer: -2 points.
  • ★ Failing to answer after buzzing: -1 point.
  • ★ 4 teams will be eliminated in this round.

📍 Round 3: Rapid Fire

  • ★ 2 minutes per team to answer as many questions as possible.
  • ★ Correct answer: +1 point.
  • ★ Incorrect answer: -1 point.
  • ★ Teams can say "Skip" to move to the next question.
  • ★ The team with the highest score wins.
