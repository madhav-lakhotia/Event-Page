Canva Poster Making

About the Event

The Canva Poster Making competition invites participants to unleash their creativity and showcase artistic ingenuity by designing posters using Canva.

Rules & Guidelines

  • ⭐ Participants will compete **individually**.
  • ⭐ Topics will be **revealed on the spot** via a chit system.
  • ⭐ **30 minutes** will be given to complete the poster.
  • ⭐ Posters must be created **exclusively using Canva**.
  • ⭐ The winner will be decided based on **creativity, uniqueness, and design quality**.
  • ⭐ **Copying or stealing** work will result in **immediate disqualification**.
